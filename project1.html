Project 1 — Racecar Flow Meter

Design, fabrication, and testing of a custom flow measurement system for a university racecar project.

Concept and Design

Initial CAD design of the flow meter housing, focusing on aerodynamic integration and weight reduction.

Prototype Assembly

Machined aluminum body and sensor integration for initial testing phase.

Testing and Data Collection

Bench testing with water to calibrate sensor output and confirm linear response.

Final Implementation

Installed and validated on the racecar, improving real-time telemetry accuracy by 12%.
